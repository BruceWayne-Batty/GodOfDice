In ancient times, two gods, Themis and Chaos, waged an unending war that shattered the very world they sought to control. Their conflict raged on, leaving the world fractured and broken.
In their final moments, both gods entrusted their essence to powerful divine artifacts, hoping that one day someone would wield them and restore them to life.
Now, rumors swirl of some mysterious artifacts emerging from the shadows. It is said that whoever claims these artifacts will gain the power to rule the world and resurrect one of the fallen gods.
You are among the faithful, driven by the belief that these artifacts hold the key to unimaginable power and the revival of a deity.
